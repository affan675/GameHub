How to Play Maze Paint

  • Goal: Paint all reachable empty cells on the board.
  • Movement: Use the Arrow Keys to slide the ball.
  • Mouse Control: Click on any paintable cell in the same row or column to slide directly to it.
  • Sliding: Once the ball moves, it will not stop until it hits a wall, the edge of the grid, or the cell you clicked.
  • Painting: Every cell the ball passes over changes color.
